How We Work

Simple but efficient proposal roadmap. All of us find each other in the way.

INTRO Acquire Problem

We both take time to catch each other by understanding problem and seeking for solution together. After communication via email/chat/meeting, Teamlizer propose solution design (included system architecture and wireframe if needed), release timeline, resource distribution and financial plan.

VERSE Contract Deal

Giving consider time, both side get back and optimize plan before sign contract.

CHORUS Agile Craft Cycle

Agreement done, time to kick off. We do two-week sprints and follow Agile methodology to ensure we both on the same direction.

OUTRO Close to Open

After finite loop on time, desired product released which route to project closed. However, Teamlizer always do the best to open next journey. After work, we are friends.
